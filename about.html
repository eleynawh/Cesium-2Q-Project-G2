about the sophist bulletin

the sophist bulletin aims not to just inform you, but to open your eyes.

the sophist bulletin is a student-made news outlet dedicated to delivering honest, well-researched, and reliable information to its readers. More than just reporting headlines, it strives to present global events and noteworthy achievements with clarity, depth, and accountability. By combining factual reporting with thoughtful analysis, the sophist bulletin aims to help its audience stay informed, think critically, and remain aware of the ever-changing world around them. Its mission is not only to inform but also to inspire a broader understanding of society, encouraging readers to engage with current issues and recognize the stories that shape our future. For the thinkers, to the doers.

the Sophist Bulletin consists of 5 members each with their own task at servicing the public with trusted and reliable news articles, carefully researched and obtained via trusted sources from the various sources.

Main contributers: Eleina Laguerder, Kent Abellanosa, Vouyen Majani, Mark Jimenea, & Francis Moreno
